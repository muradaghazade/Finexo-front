About

The company Auto Import Baku was founded in 2018. The main direction of our company activities is the import of cars from various countries around the world (mostly from the USA, Canada, the Republic of Korea, China) to the Republic of Azerbaijan, as well as to other countries. Auto Import Baku provides the purchase and insured delivery of cars from all major auctions in the USA and Canada (Copart, Iaai, Manheim), as well as from showrooms, sales centers, and dealer networks.

Due to the diversity of our activities, we have the opportunity to purchase cars not only from the USA but also from domestic auctions in Korea.

In accordance with the requirements of the new era, Auto Import Baku provides the purchase and insured delivery of new hybrid, electric, and plug-in hybrid cars from various countries, mainly from China. Since 2018, our company has successfully imported more than 1600 cars to Azerbaijan.

Unlike many other companies in this industry in Azerbaijan, we offer engine and transmission insurance for the cars we purchase.
